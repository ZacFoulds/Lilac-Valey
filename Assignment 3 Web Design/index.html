About

In the heart of the wonderful Blue Mountains you will find our luxurious rustic farm stay. The farm stay was purchased by a local interior designer (Marina Ye) about three years ago and has turned the old run down barn into a breath taking get away. The farm surrounded by the amazing scenery of the blue mountains and the adorable farm animals. With key features such as multiple bedrooms, a plunge pool and an eye catching cottage garden. This is one holiday stay that you are sure to want to book a stay in.

Location



Description:

Located roughly 1.5 hours northwest of the Sydney CBD in the heart of the spectacular Blue Mountains. Within The Blue Mountains there are many places to go for a walk on the trails, go to the amazing scenic lookouts. The mountains are a host of a diverse range of activities such as going the Wentworth falls and going into the Jenolan Caves.

Things to do in Local area:

The range of activities to do outside of the homestay is endless with hikes and adventures, lots of places to eat and drink. even golf courses for those who like to play while they're on a holiday. We recommend doing further research online to see what you would like to do while staying at our amazing farm stay.
